About the Visualization

Plot: The magnitude of the normalized total electric field vector is shown in the \(xy\)-plane over one complete period of oscillation. The polarization state is described by following vector:

\(\textbf{Z}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{E_{1}^{2} + E_{2}^{2}}} \begin{pmatrix} E_{1}e^{i\phi_{1}} \\ E_{2}e^{i\phi_{2}} \end{pmatrix}\)

Sliders: Vary the following parameters: \(E_{1}\), \(E_{2}\), \(\phi_{1}\), and \(\phi_{2}\).
